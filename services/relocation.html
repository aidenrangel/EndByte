Relocation & Migration

Moving a data center is a thousand small risks riding in the same truck. EndByte plans, transports, and re-commissions your environment with serial-level tracking from the old floor to the new one.

The move is the easy part. The plan is everything.

A successful relocation is decided before anything is unplugged. We start with a full inventory and dependency map, sequence the shutdown to minimize downtime windows, and stage the destination site so equipment goes from truck to rack without sitting on a dock.

In transit, every asset is tracked by serial against the inventory. At the destination, our deployment crews re-rack, re-cable, and bring the environment back up to the documented plan — the same teams that handle our greenfield deployments.

If part of the environment isn't making the move, it flows into our decommissioning and asset recovery processes instead — one project plan covers the whole transition, with nothing falling between vendors.
