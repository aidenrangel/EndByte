On-Site Destruction

For some data, leaving the building isn't an option. We bring the destruction to you — performed at your facility, witnessed by your people, certified on the spot.

Your data never leaves your custody

Plenty of security policies — and plenty of reasonable security instincts — prohibit data-bearing media from leaving the premises intact. On-site destruction solves that cleanly: the drives are sanitized or destroyed before they ever pass your door.

EndByte schedules a window, sets up at your facility, and works through your media with your team free to witness every step. Serial numbers are logged on the spot, and certificates document exactly what happened to each device, where, and when.

It's the same standard as our bench work — NIST 800-88 wiping or physical destruction — just performed inside your own four walls.
