Data Destruction

When data has to be gone, “deleted” isn't a standard — verified is. EndByte sanitizes storage media by software wipe or physical destruction, and documents both to the same audit-ready standard.

Two paths to provably gone

Software sanitization is our default for healthy drives. Using KillDisk Industrial across a dedicated wipe bench, we run multi-pass overwrites to NIST 800-88 and DoD 5220.22-M standards, then perform a full-surface read-back to verify every sector returns zero. Wiped drives keep their resale value — which is what makes our free destruction program possible.

Physical destruction handles everything else: drives that fail verification, failed or degraded media that can't be reliably wiped, and any media your policy requires destroyed regardless of condition. Shredding and hydraulic crushing reduce drives to fragments that no laboratory recovers data from.

Either path ends the same way — with a per-drive certificate listing the serial number, method used, date, and verification result. Your auditors get a complete, defensible record; you get to stop thinking about those drives forever.
