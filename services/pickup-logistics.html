Secure Pickup & Chain of Custody

The riskiest part of destroying data is moving it. Our pickup service puts every drive on the record before it moves and keeps it there until it's certified gone.

Documented from your door to destruction

A chain of custody is only as strong as its weakest handoff — and most breaches of process happen in transit, not at the destination. That's why our pickup isn't a courier run; it's the first step of the destruction process.

Media is inventoried by serial number at your site before anything moves, sealed for transport, and signed at every handoff. From the moment we take possession, every drive is accounted for against the intake list — and that list reconciles exactly against the certificates you receive at the end.

For Bay Area clients, pickup-to-certificate typically runs about 24 hours. If your policy doesn't allow media off-site at all, our on-site destruction service covers that instead.
