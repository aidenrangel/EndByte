Data Center Decommissioning

Shutting down a data center is a project with a thousand details and zero tolerance for lost drives. EndByte manages the entire teardown — planning through disposal — under one accountable team.

From live floor to empty room, on schedule

A decommission touches everything: power-down sequencing, asset inventory, data-bearing device handling, de-racking, cable recovery, transport logistics, and final disposition. Done piecemeal across multiple vendors, the chain of custody fractures and accountability disappears into the gaps.

EndByte runs the project end to end. We start with a planning phase that maps every rack and asset, sequence the power-down to protect your remaining operations, then de-rack, inventory, and remove equipment with serial-level tracking. Data-bearing devices flow directly into our certified destruction process — no third-party handoff, no unaccounted drives.

What leaves the building is documented. What had data on it is certified destroyed. What still has value comes back to you as recovered budget through our asset recovery program.
