Free Certified Destruction

Certified destruction with a zero-dollar invoice. The trade is simple and stated up front: we keep the drives after they're wiped and verified, and the service costs you nothing.

How free actually works

There's no catch, just an aligned trade. Enterprise drives that have been properly sanitized and verified still hold hardware value. When you let EndByte retain the drives after wiping, that recovered value pays for the service — so we don't need to invoice you for it.

You get the full process, not a lite version: serial-level intake, multi-pass NIST 800-88 wiping, complete read-back verification, and a certificate for every drive. Drives that fail verification are physically destroyed and documented the same way — they just don't have resale value, which is our problem, not yours.

The program fits best with working enterprise drives in reasonable volume. If your media is mostly failed, exotic, or policy-bound to physical destruction, we'll tell you straight and quote the paid service instead.
