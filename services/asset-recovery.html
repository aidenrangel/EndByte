Asset Recovery

Retired hardware isn't waste — it's an asset class your books forgot about. We test, grade, and remarket end-of-life equipment so it comes back to you as budget instead of a disposal invoice.

Idle racks are stranded capital

Servers, switches, drives, and infrastructure that no longer serve your environment still hold real market value — but only if they're tested, graded, and sold through the right channels before that value decays. Most organizations either pay to dispose of it or let it depreciate in a storage cage.

EndByte handles the whole recovery cycle. Equipment is functionally tested and cosmetically graded; data-bearing devices are sanitized and certified through our destruction process first, so nothing leaves with your data on it. Then we remarket through established secondary-market channels to maximize realized value.

The result: a documented return on hardware you'd already written off, with the same chain-of-custody rigor as everything else we do.
