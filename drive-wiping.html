Secure Drive Wiping

Software sanitization done to a standard, not a vibe — multi-pass overwrites, full-surface verification, and a certificate proving every sector reads zero.

Wiped, verified, and proven

Wiping a drive properly means more than running a tool and trusting it. EndByte runs multi-pass overwrites to NIST 800-88 and DoD 5220.22-M standards across a dedicated wipe bench, with every drive monitored through every pass.

Then comes the part most providers skip: a full-surface read-back of every sector. If anything other than zeros comes back, the drive fails — and failed drives don't get a second chance, they get physically destroyed. Only drives that pass complete verification earn a certificate.

Because wiped drives keep their hardware value, software sanitization is also what powers our free destruction program — if we keep the verified drives afterward, the entire service can cost you nothing.
